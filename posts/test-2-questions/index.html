Test 2 Questions

Below are the questions for our second test. The test will consist of some of the questions on this list. Expect six of them. The questions selected for the test will appear on the test exactly as they are written here (with the sole exception of corrections like spelling errors).

For each question, write at least five sentences, and perhaps more. Where an argument is sought, consider presenting it as a list of numbered sentences rather than in a paragraph. Write as though you’re explaining the answer to another student who is not in the course (as opposed to discussing it with someone who is already familiar with the course material).

When the following list represents the complete list of questions for the test, this paragraph will be changed to say so; as of now, this list is incomplete.
