Topic 9: Meaning of Life

Does anything anchor what we should aim for in life? Does anything make life meaningful? How could we know? And what would be the alternative? Nihilism—that nothing matters? Or would life without an anchored meaning be absurd and pointless?

Reading

  1. Robert Nozick, “The Experience Machine” excerpt from Anarchy, State, and Utopia (1974), the first part of this
  2. Susan Wolf, “The Meanings of Lives” (2015) from The Variety of Values: Essays on Morality, Meaning, and Love, available here.
  3. Thomas Nagel, “The Absurd” (1971) The Journal of Philosophy 68(20), available here through JSTOR.
